Locations

We will be married in the town hall at Monte San Savino, Arezzo at 4pm on Saturday, September 10th 2016.

This will be followed by a reception at the Castello de Rapolano in the town of Serre di Rapolano, Siena.

Transport

There are a large number of options for travelling to the celebration.

We recommend flying to Perugia, Florence or Pisa and hiring a car there, but Bologna and Rome airports are also easily accessible.

Accommodation

The venue for the reception, the Castello di Rapolano, provides wonderful accommodation, with space for many of our guests if they are staying both Friday and Saturday nights.

Alternatively, there are several alternative B&Bs in the same town of Serre di Rapolano.

Wedding Presents

To coin a cliché, your presence is enough of a present, but there are several charities to which we would love you to donate if you'd like to do something to mark the occasion. Nevertheless, if you are desperate to buy us an extravagant series of priceless gifts, we will grudgingly accept them.
