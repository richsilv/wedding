Transport

Getting to and from Tuscany

Air travel

Whilst the closest city to Serre di Rapolano, Siena, has no international airport, it is within easy reach of a variety of other cities which are served by international flights.

Travel from the north of England

Inexplicably, there appears to be some sort of travel embargo on people travelling from the north to Tuscany. The flights below are the best options for travelling to the wedding, but you could also consider flying from Stansted, which is easily accessible from the A1 and East Coast mainline.

Travel from further afield

If you're travelling from somewhere outside the UK directly to Italy, Rome will be best served with international flights, and is less than three hours from the venue by road or rail.

Onward from the airport

The easiest option to reach Serre di Rapolano would be to hire a car at the airport you're flying into, or else to share with other guests who are doing so. Due to its rural location, there is no station in the town itself.

Alternatively, regular trains run from Rome (3h) and Pisa (2h30m) to Rapolano Terme, which is only a ten minute drive from Serre di Rapolano, and from which you could reach the venue by taxi or a lift from a friendly guest.

Train information and bookings can be found at Trenitalia.
